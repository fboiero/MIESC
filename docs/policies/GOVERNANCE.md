## Appendix: Academic Origin and Project Phase

*Last updated: June 2026*

MIESC began as part of a Master's thesis in Cyberdefense at Universidad de la
Defensa Nacional (UNDEF), Argentina, and has since shipped as a maintained
open-source product (current release: v5.4.3).

| Phase | Model | Window |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Origin | Single maintainer (thesis phase) | 2024 – early 2026 |
| **Current** | **Maintained release + community growth** (BDFL with open contribution) | **2026 –** |
| Future | Core team formation, then meritocratic steering committee | as the contributor base grows |
